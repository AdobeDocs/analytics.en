# Ad Hoc Analysis Project Converter overview

>[!IMPORTANT]
>
>Adobe is moving Ad Hoc Analysis to end of life on March 1, 2021. [Learn more](https://adobe.ly/discoverworkspace)

The Ad Hoc Analysis Project Converter seamlessly converts your Ad Hoc Analysis projects to [Analysis Workspace](https://docs.adobe.com/content/help/en/analytics/analyze/analysis-workspace/home.html). Projects can be converted one at a time, or in bulk.

# Restore deleted FTP or SFTP accounts and data

Any data that has been deleted from an FTP or SFTP account cannot be restored by Adobe.

If an FTP or SFTP account that you had been intending to use has been disabled, contact your Adobe Account Team, who can discuss additional options with you.

# Contact and legal Information

For information to help you contact Adobe and to understand the legal issues concerning your use of this product and documentation:

## Help & technical Support

The Adobe Experience Cloud Customer Care team can assist you with your Regional Data Collection (RDC) issues. To get their help:


* Check the [Experience Cloud help](https://helpx.adobe.com/support/marketing-cloud.html) pages for advice, tips, and FAQs.
* Ask a quick question on Twitter [@AdobeMktgCare](https://twitter.com/AdobeMktgCare).
* [Log an incident in the customer portal](https://helpx.adobe.com/contact/enterprise-support.html#marketing-cloud).
* Contact the [Customer Care] team (https://helpx.adobe.com/contact/enterprise-support.ec.html)directly.
* Check availability and [status](https://status.adobe.com/) of Experience Cloud Solutions.

## Service, capability & billing.

Depending on your solution configuration, some options described in this documentation might not be available to you. As each account is unique, please refer to your contract for pricing, due dates, terms, and conditions. If you would like to add to or otherwise change your service level, or if you have questions regarding your current service, please contact your Account Manager.
